Background Information

Maintenance Matic Ltd, (MM Ltd) is a general contractor who supplies and Installs quality power systems and equipment where businesses and people flourish. Our Engineering Company offers extensive services, designs and installation fittings to meet customer needs. In addition to being general commercial and industrial contractor, MM Ltd also provides Electrical Installation, Electrical Survey, Solar power, Power backup Systems, Diesel Generators and Air conditioning together with Service and Maintenance.
