SR Market - E-Commerce Platform

April 2026 | E-Commerce, Web Development

Overview

SR Market is a comprehensive e-commerce marketplace platform designed to provide businesses with a complete online selling solution. Built with modern web technologies, it offers seamless inventory management, secure payment processing, and an intuitive user experience for both sellers and buyers.

Key Features

  • Product catalog management with categories and search functionality
  • Shopping cart and wishlist capabilities
  • Secure checkout flow with multiple payment options
  • Order tracking and management dashboard
  • Customer reviews and ratings system
  • Mobile-responsive design for on-the-go shopping

Technical Approach

The platform leverages modern frontend frameworks for a responsive, fast-loading experience. The architecture prioritizes scalability to handle growing product catalogs and increasing traffic. Integration with payment gateways ensures secure transactions while maintaining a smooth checkout experience.

SR Garage - Vehicle Management System

April 2026 | Business Management, Automotive

Overview

SR Garage is a specialized management application built for automotive businesses. It streamlines garage operations by providing digital solutions for vehicle tracking, service scheduling, customer management, and billing - replacing traditional paper-based systems with a modern, efficient alternative.

Key Features

  • Vehicle registration and history tracking
  • Service appointment scheduling calendar
  • Job card and work order management
  • Parts inventory and supplier tracking
  • Customer database with contact management
  • Invoice generation and payment tracking
  • Service reminders and notifications

Target Users

SR Garage serves independent auto repair shops, multi-bay garages, and automotive service centers looking to digitize their operations. The intuitive interface requires minimal training, making the transition from paper records effortless for staff of all technical levels.

SR Butchery - Meat Business Solution

April 2026 | Retail, Inventory Management

Overview

SR Butchery is a specialized inventory and sales management system designed specifically for butchery businesses. It addresses the unique challenges of fresh meat retail - from tracking perishable inventory and managing weight-based pricing to handling bulk orders and maintaining quality standards.

Key Features

  • Weight-based product pricing and calculations
  • Real-time inventory tracking with expiry monitoring
  • Supplier and purchase order management
  • Sales recording with daily/weekly reports
  • Customer loyalty program integration
  • Low stock alerts and reorder reminders
  • Multi-branch support for larger operations

Why It Matters

Traditional butchery management often relies on handwritten notes and manual calculations. SR Butchery brings accuracy and efficiency by automating weight calculations, tracking inventory in real-time, and generating insights that help business owners reduce waste and increase profitability.

SR Payroll - Salary Management System

April 2026 | HR, Finance, Automation

Overview

SR Payroll is a comprehensive payroll application designed to simplify salary management for businesses of all sizes. From calculating deductions and taxes to generating pay slips and reports, the system automates the complex payroll process while ensuring accuracy and compliance.

Key Features

  • Employee database management
  • Automated salary calculations with custom rules
  • Tax deduction calculations and reporting
  • Allowance and benefit tracking

  • Monthly and annual payroll reports
  • Leave and attendance integration

SR Restaurant - Hospitality Management

April 2026 | Hospitality, Food & Beverage

Overview

SR Restaurant is a full-stack restaurant management system that handles everything from order taking and table management to kitchen display integration and billing. Built for the fast-paced restaurant environment, it helps hospitality businesses deliver faster service while reducing operational errors.

Key Features

  • Digital menu with categories and modifiers
  • Table management with status tracking
  • Order management from placement to fulfillment
  • Kitchen display system (KDS) integration

Gang Life Sim - Urban Life Simulation

April 2026 | Game Development, Simulation

Overview

Gang Life Sim is an immersive life simulation game set in an urban gangster environment. Players navigate the complexities of street life - making choices, building relationships, managing resources, and experiencing consequences. It's a narrative-driven experience that explores themes of loyalty, ambition, and survival.

Gameplay Features

  • Open-world urban environment exploration
  • Character progression and skill development
  • Relationship system with NPCs
  • Economic management and money-making ventures
  • Decision-based narrative with multiple outcomes
  • Reputation and territory mechanics
  • Dynamic events and missions

Development Notes

Built using web technologies for accessibility, Gang Life Sim demonstrates that browser-based games can deliver engaging narrative experiences. The project showcases skills in game loop implementation, state management, and creating meaningful player choices that affect the game world.
